FAQ

Common questions about tracking chores, premium, reminders, and how LogMe works.

What is LogMe for?

LogMe is for tracking recurring chores and everyday tasks with a single tap. It helps you remember what happened, review history, and understand patterns over time — so nothing slips through the cracks.

How do I log an event?

On the home screen, tap the log button on an event card. LogMe records the timestamp immediately. You can also view and delete past logs from the event detail screen.

How do reminders work?

If you enable nudges for an event, LogMe uses your past logs to estimate when it's likely due again and sends a local notification one day before. The app needs at least two logs before it can make a useful prediction.

Why doesn't my event show a prediction yet?

LogMe needs at least two logs to calculate an average interval. Once you have two or more entries, the next expected date and chart will appear automatically.

Is my data stored online?

No. All event and log history is stored locally on your device using SQLite. The app does not require an account and does not upload your tracking data anywhere.

What does premium unlock?

The free plan supports up to 3 events. Premium unlocks unlimited events so you can track as many chores and routines as you like. It's a single annual subscription billed through the App Store.

How do I restore a purchase?

Open Settings and tap "Restore Purchase". If your App Store account already has an active subscription, LogMe will unlock premium automatically.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your device to cancel at any time. You'll keep premium access until the end of your current billing period.
