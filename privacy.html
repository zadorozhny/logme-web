Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how LogMe handles information when you use the app and related web pages.

Last updated: April 28, 2026

1. Summary

LogMe is designed to keep your tracking data simple and private. Event names, emojis, and log history are stored locally on your device. LogMe does not require account creation to use its core features.

2. Information we process

  • Event information you create, such as event names and emojis.
  • Event history and timestamps for the logs you record.
  • Notification preference choices, including whether nudges are enabled.
  • Purchase and entitlement status needed to unlock premium features.
  • Support communications if you email us directly.

3. How your data is used

  • To display your event list, history, and statistics inside the app.
  • To calculate trends such as intervals and monthly activity.
  • To schedule optional local notifications or nudges when you enable them.
  • To determine whether a premium purchase is active.
  • To respond to customer support requests you send to us.

4. Local device storage

Event and log history are stored locally on your device using SQLite and related local app storage. Your core usage data stays on the device unless you choose to share it through your own device tools.

5. Notifications

If you enable nudges, LogMe requests notification permission from the operating system. If permission is granted, the app may schedule local notifications based on your event history.

You can turn notifications off at any time either inside the app or through your device settings.

6. Payments and subscriptions

Premium purchases are processed by the platform provider (Apple or Google), and purchase entitlement status is handled through RevenueCat. We use that information only to determine access to premium functionality and to support restore-purchases behavior.

We do not store your payment card number inside the app.

7. Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may rely on service providers that help operate app functionality, such as app-store billing or purchase entitlement infrastructure, but only to the extent needed to provide the service.

8. Data retention

Local app data remains on your device until you delete it, remove the app, or clear app data. Support emails may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to you and maintain support records.

9. Children's privacy

LogMe is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us directly, contact us and we will review the request.

10. Your choices

  • You can edit or delete events and logs inside the app.
  • You can disable nudges or revoke notification permission in device settings.
  • You can manage premium subscriptions through your app-store account settings.
  • You can stop using the app and remove it from your device at any time.

11. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the app and related services, but no method of storage or transmission is completely secure. You use the app at your own risk.

12. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date on this page.

13. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at hellologme@gmail.com.
